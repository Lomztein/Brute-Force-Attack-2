 - Difficulty aspects - 
Enemy damage - allows multiplication of how much damage enemies does.
	50% on Easy
	150% on Hard
	200% on Brutal
Resource multipliers - Adds a multiplier to incoming resources
	125% on Easy



Research cost - Adds a multiplier to research costs.
Research time - Adds a flat increase / multiplier to research time.
Structure cost - Adds a multiplier to structure costs.
Max research options - Limits the choices the player has when selecting research.
Max simultanious research - Limits how much research can be ongoing at once. Only affects timed or unique requirements research.
Wave multiplier - Allows the multiplication of wave length / enemy amount.
